I'm New Here

Welcome! We're glad you're considering a visit.

What to Expect

Here's what you can expect when you visit us for the first time

Service Length

Our services typically last about 75 minutes, including worship, announcements, and the sermon.

Worship Style

Our worship blends contemporary and traditional elements. We sing both modern worship songs and classic hymns.

Teaching Style

Our messages are biblically-based, practical, and applicable to everyday life. We focus on how Scripture speaks to our current challenges.

What to Wear

We want you to feel comfortable when you visit. You'll find people dressed in everything from casual (jeans and t-shirts) to business casual (slacks and button-ups or dresses).

Our priority is that you join us, not what you wear. Come as you are, and know you'll be welcomed warmly!

For Families with Children

We love children and believe they are an important part of our church family. Here's what we offer for kids:

  • Nursery (0-2 years): Safe, loving care for our littlest ones during all services.
  • Preschool (3-5 years): Age-appropriate activities, Bible stories, and fun during the 10:30 AM service.
  • Elementary (K-5th grade): Interactive lessons, games, and worship during the 10:30 AM service.

All our children's ministry volunteers undergo background checks and are trained to provide a safe, nurturing environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from first-time visitors

Where do I go when I arrive?

When you arrive, look for our welcome team members wearing blue name tags. They'll greet you, help you check in your children if needed, and direct you to the sanctuary or any other areas you'd like to visit.

Will I be asked to stand up or introduce myself?

No, we won't single you out or ask you to stand up. We want you to feel comfortable, not put on the spot. We do have a welcome card in the service bulletin if you'd like to share your information with us.

Is there a coffee hour or fellowship time?

Yes! We have coffee, tea, and light refreshments available before and after each service in our fellowship hall. It's a great opportunity to meet people and ask any questions you might have.

Do you have facilities for nursing mothers?

Yes, we have a comfortable, private nursing mothers' room adjacent to the nursery. It's equipped with changing facilities, comfortable seating, and a live audio feed of the service.

How do I get connected beyond Sunday services?

The best way to get connected is through our small groups and ministry teams. Visit our welcome center after the service, and we'll help you find the right fit. You can also attend our "New to Church Name" class, offered monthly.

Ready to Visit?

We look forward to meeting you this Sunday. If you have any questions before your visit, please don't hesitate to contact us.
