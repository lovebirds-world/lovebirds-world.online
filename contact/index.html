Getting Here

By Car

We have a large parking lot with plenty of free parking spaces, including accessible parking near the main entrance.

Public Transportation

Bus lines 10 and 14 stop directly in front of our building. The nearest subway station is Central Station, a 10-minute walk from our church.

Accessibility

Our building is fully accessible with ramp access at the main entrance and elevator access to all floors. Accessible restrooms are available on each floor.
